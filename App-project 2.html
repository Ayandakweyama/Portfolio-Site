PetSure Petcare App

Comprehensive Mobile Application

The PetSure Petcare App is a comprehensive mobile application designed for pet owners to manage their pet's health and wellness needs effectively. The app provides features such as vaccination schedules, appointment bookings with veterinarians, pet health tracking, and reminders for medication. Developed using Jetpack Compose (Android Studio), the app ensures a modern and intuitive user interface. The project focuses on delivering a seamless experience for users, combining accessibility and robust functionality.
