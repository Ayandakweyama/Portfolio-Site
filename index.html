About Me

A little bit about Me

Hi, I'm Ayanda Kweyama, a passionate software developer and graphic designer with a deep curiosity about the intersection of design, technology, and optimization. My journey into coding was inspired by my love for graphic design, which ignited questions about how logos, branding, websites, apps, and workplace systems could be optimized to enhance functionality and user experience.

As a student at the University of Mpumalanga, I have developed expertise in building responsive and dynamic web applications. My projects range from creating e-commerce platforms tailored to university faculties to integrating seamless payment solutions like PayFast. I work with a diverse tech stack, including Django, Node.js, Vue.js, Java EE, PHP, Python, Java, Flutter, React, Android Studio, and a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am also proficient in database technologies such as MySQL and MongoDB.

Beyond coding, I am a writer and visionary. My upcoming book, MuAfrika, explores the concept of Africa adopting a centralized cryptocurrency to transform its economy. The book will be released in mid-January on Play Store Books and Amazon, reflecting my commitment to merging technology with economic innovation to empower the continent.

On the design front, I am highly skilled in platforms like Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, and Canva, which I initially embraced to digitize sketches of logos and artwork. My passion for design continues to influence my approach to creating user-centric and aesthetically pleasing digital solutions.

Whether through elegant code, innovative ideas, or creative design, my mission is to bridge gaps and transform lives through technology.
